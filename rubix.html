Welcome to Rubix

Rubix evolves Rubik’s Cube solutions.

It improves on a published evolutionary Thistlethwaite algorithm through innovations including geometric selection, shared prepended linked-list move histories and an expanded three-part final phase.

Rubix solves in 3.8 seconds versus 340 without sacrificing on solution length (49.9 moves versus 50.3). Rubix’s averages are from 10,000 runs in Safari 9.0.0 on a mid-2013 Macbook Air (i7, 8 gb, OS X 10.11).
